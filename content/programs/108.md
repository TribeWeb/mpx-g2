# FuzzWah+

Loads with a compressor on and a fuzz wah just waiting to be activated by the MPX R1 `Toe Switch`. The MPX R1 pedal controls the wah; the `Toe Switch` simultaneously turns gain and wah on or off. `A/B` adds chorus and echo.
