# Rotary Cab

A Leslie with a bit of chamber to fatten up the sound. Press `Gain` to add a little grit, so when you dig in you get some distortion. `A/B` controls fast and slow speeds of the rotary speaker. The MPX R1 `Toe Switch` activates pedal control of volume or wah.
