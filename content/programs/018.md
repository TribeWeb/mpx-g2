# Stomp!

A collection of classic stomp boxes just waiting to be activated. Press `Gain` for a Screamer, `Effect 1` for a Univybe, `Effect 2` for a Wah, `Chorus` for a Flanger, `Delay` for Echo and `Reverb` for a vintage plate. `A/B` increases the delay and reverb mix. `Tap` changes delay time. The MPX R1 `Toe Switch` activates pedal control of volume or wah.
