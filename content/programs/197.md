# Vibro Cab

The original Vibro Tone guitar cabinet had a rotating baffle in front of a 12 inch guitar speaker. It produced a swirling sound by spraying the speaker output through the top and sides of the cabinet as the baffle rotated. A built-in crossover sent high frequencies back to the original guitar amp so that the top end sound didn’t swirl. We use a crossover, stereo chorus and auto panners to create the effect. `A/B` toggles between slow and fast rotary speeds
