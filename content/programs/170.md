# LiveJimi Classic

Hendrix tone with a Univybe and a plate reverb. Press `A/B` to shut off the reverb and add an Octabuzz effect and a slapback echo for some classic Hendrix tones. The MPX R1 pedal controls volume, the `Toe Switch` turns the pedal on and off.
