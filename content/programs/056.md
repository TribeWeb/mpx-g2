# Round Trem

A classic combination of vintage and modern effects. In front of the amp there’s a tempo controlled tremolo with a vintage vibe. `Delay` and reverb are in the effects loop. If you want even more shimmer, turn on `Effect 1`. (It’s running a dual pitch shifter, tweaked for fat detune). The pedal controls the depth of the tremolo.
