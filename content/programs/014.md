# Pedal Swell

Compression with chorus and delay that go into a panner. A truly stereo effect. `A/B` changes delay feedback time from 20 to 80%. `Tap` sets delay time. The MPX R1 pedal controls volume to let you create ethereal volume swells. The `Toe Switch` turns the pedal on and off.
