# JamMan

It's baaaack!!!!!!! A medium-gain preamp with a chorus, reverb and a 20-second single loop JamMan! The MPX G2 front panel buttons or the MPX R1 control: `Tap` = Record Loop, `A/B` = Layer, `Delay` = Clear Loop. The MPX R1 `Toe Switch` lets the pedal control volume (`Toe Switch` off) or wah (`Toe Switch` on).
