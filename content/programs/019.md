# OctaWah

This rig lets you switch from a standard vintage echo and plate reverb to a full-on Screamer with Octabuzz and Wah. The MPX R1 `Toe Switch` simultaneously adds gain, `EQ`, Wah and Octabuzz for a classic Hendrix sound. When the pedal is not controlling the wah, its acts as a power amp volume pedal. `A/B` adds more delay and reverb. `Tap` sets the echo time.
