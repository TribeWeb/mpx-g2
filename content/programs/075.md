# Modern Trem

A modern (alternative style) tremolo with a sharper, square wave type feel than a vintage tremolo. 76 `Tap` Echo `Tap` sets the echo rhythm. `A/B` turns the input to the echo on and off (so that the repeats ring out when you turn it off). The MPX R1 pedal sets the loudness of the echoes. Press `Soft Row` for instant access to all of the delay parameters.The program loads with delay time set to 2 echoes/beat (8th note). Be sure to experiment with different values.
