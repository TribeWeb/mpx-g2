# TechnoChords

Play a chord and let it ring out. Random tones ring out into delay and reverb. `A/B` changes the mix of the effect so that more of the original signal can be heard. `Tap` changes delay time and LFO speeds controlling a sweep filter. The MPX R1 `Toe Switch` gives you pedal control of volume or of volume with a sweep filter scale.
