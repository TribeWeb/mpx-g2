# Metal Stack

Get out your heaviest chops and burn. Super high gain setting with lots of tone and just a hint of ambience. `A/B` changes the sound from dirty to clean and adds more reverb. The MPX R1 `Toe Switch` activates pedal wah. 157. Pdl Octaves Use a pedal to create outrageous octave sweeps. `A/B` changes the direction of the sweep (A=octave up, B=octave down). `Chorus`, delay and reverb are in the effects loop. Check out the Glide parameter in the `Soft Row`. When it’s set to Off, the pedal prodcues a chromatic glissando instead of a continuous glide.
