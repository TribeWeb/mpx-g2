# Rotary Cab

A Leslie speaker has separate rotating elements for treble and bass. Our version adds Chamber reverb to put the cab in in a small room. The preamp sound is clean with a little bit of grit, so when you dig in you get some distortion. `A/B` button controls fast and slow speeds of the rotary speaker.
