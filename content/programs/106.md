# EnvFilter LP

A very funky, synth-like effect, reminiscent of a Mu-tron III set to “LP”. The dynamics of your playing opens and closes a resonant filter. Change the Scale parameter in the `Soft Row` to adjust to the output differences of different guitars and basses.
