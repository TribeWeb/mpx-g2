# EQ Delay

A mono 20-second delay with 2-band parameteric `EQ` in the feedback path. In this program the `EQ` is set to simulate the frequency roll-offs associated with analog tape, but you can create all sorts of unusual echo effects by varying the `EQ` parameters. Everything you need is in the `Soft Row`. (Be careful with `EQ` `Gain` settings when using delay feedback — in general, it’s best to cut `EQ` `Gain` rather than boost it.)
