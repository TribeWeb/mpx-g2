# DynaChrsTrem

Another combination of old and new. First, there’s a RedComp compressor in front of the preamp for stomp box style sustain. Then we’ve added a reverb followed by tremolo plus stereo chorus and delay in the effects loop. Use the pedal to find just the right tremolo rate.
