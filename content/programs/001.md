# G2 Blue

This is one heck of a rig — our take on the Cry Baby wah, Uni Vibe and Tube Screamer - all tweaked up to drive a guitar amp running with lots of gain. Use the MPX R1 Toe Switch to swap the Wah with the UniVybe. While the UniVybe is active, the pedal is a power amp volume control. The Screamer overdrive is bypassed when the program loads - press `Gain` to turn it on. `A/B` turns up the echo level and feedback. (`A` is dry, `B` is wet). `Tap` sets the echo rhythm.
