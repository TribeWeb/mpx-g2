# Pdl Octaves

Use a pedal to create outrageous octave sweeps. `A/B` changes the direction of the sweep (A=octave up, B=octave down). `Chorus`, delay and reverb are in the effects loop. Check out the Glide parameter in the `Soft Row`. When it's set to Off, the pedal produces a chromatic glissando instead of a continuous glide.
