# Unchained

The classic Gray Flange with the mix toned down a bit, and some delay. Press `A/B` to toggle the flange on and off like the real riff. `Tap` sets delay time. This program works best into a high-gain amp. The MPX R1 pedal controls volume, the `Toe Switch` turns the pedal on and off.
